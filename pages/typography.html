Headings

All Klasify headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available.

h1. Captain America

h2. Captain America

h3. Captain America

h4. Captain America

h5. Captain America
h6. Captain America
Example:
<h1>h1. Captain America</h1>
<h2>h2. Captain America</h2>
<h3>h3. Captain America</h3>
<h4>h4. Captain America</h4>
<h5>h5. Captain America</h5>
<h6>h6. Captain America</h6>

.h1 through .h6 classes are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but cannot use the associated Klasify element.

Example:
<p class="h1">h1. Captain America</p>
<p class="h2">h2. Captain America</p>
<p class="h3">h3. Captain America</p>
<p class="h4">h4. Captain America</p>
<p class="h5">h5. Captain America</p>
<p class="h6">h6. Captain America</p>
Lead

Make a paragraph stand out by adding .lead.

Please fill up the necessary fields.

Example:
<p class="lead">Please fill up the necessary fields.</p>
Text Alignment

Easily realign text to components with text alignment classes.

Sadron's Protracted Frisson!

Myrault's Hinder-Gast!

Killing Wall of Koryx!

Example:
<p class="text-left">Sadron's Protracted Frisson!</p>
<p class="text-center">Myrault's Hinder-Gast!</p>
<p class="text-right">Killing Wall of Koryx!</p>
Text Transform

Transform text in components with text capitalization classes.

Shimare's Extractive Pulse!

Claws of Tornarus!

Zeal of Wexort!

Example:
<p class="text-lowercase">Shimare's Extractive Pulse!</p>
<p class="text-uppercase">Claws of Tornarus!</p>
<p class="text-capitalize">Zeal of Wexort!</p>
Font Weight and Italics

Quickly change the weight (boldness) of text or italicize text.

Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!

Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!

Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!

Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!

Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!

Example:
<p class="font-thin ">Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!</p>
<p class="font-light">Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!</p>
<p class="font-medium">Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!</p>
<p class="font-bold">Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!</p>
<p class="text-small">Harlek's Incantation of Incineration!</p>
