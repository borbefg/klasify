Accordion
Accordions are useful when you want to toggle between hiding and showing large amount of content:

Batman

Superman

Green Lantern

HTML
Example:
<button class="accordion> Accordion Button </button>
 <div class="panel>
  Lorem Ipsum . . .
 </div>
<button class="accordion> Accordion Button </button>
 <div class="panel>
  Lorem Ipsum . . .
 </div>
<button class="accordion> Accordion Button </button>
 <div class="panel>
  Lorem Ipsum . . .
 </div>
Javascript
var acc = document.getElementByClassName("accordion");
var item;

for(item = 0; item < acc.length; item++){
 acc[item].onclick = function(){
  this.classList.toggle("active");
  this.nextElementSibling.classList.toggle("show");
 }
}
