Alerts

Klasify provides an easy way to create predefined alert messages:

Message Alerts

Warning! Indicates a warning that might need attention.
Danger! Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.
The battle is ours! Victory is assured! This one is in the bag!

Alerts

Warning! Indicates a warning that might need attention.
Danger! Indicates a dangerous or potentially negative action.
Our enemy is crushed and our victory assured. Strategically, this one's in the bag!
Message Alerts are created with the one of the three classes .msg-success, .msg-alert, .msg-warningwhile normal Alerts uses .success, .error, .warning:

Example:
Message Alerts:
<div class="msg-success">
  Don't worry, lads, this one's in the bag!
</div>
<div class="msg-alert">
  Sorry, friend, I let you down.
</div>
<div class="msg-warning">
  Aw no, you've got to be kidding me.
</div>
Alerts:
<div class="success">
  It falls to me to inform you that this one is in the bag!
</div>
<div class="warning">
  Sorry, friend, I let you down.
</div>
<div class="error">
  Aw no, you've got to be kidding me.
</div>
