Button Groups

Group a series of buttons together on a single line with the button group.

Example:
<div class="btn-group">
   <button class="button-default color-white">Left</button>
   <button class="button-default color-white">Middle</button>
   <button class="button-default color-white">Right</button>
</div>

By adding .active and .disabled classes to the buttons will change its state to an active or disabled button.

Example:
<div class="btn-group">
   <button class="button-default color-white">Left</button>
   <button class="button-default color-white disabled">Middle</button>
   <button class="button-default color-white active">Right</button>
</div>
