Headings

The h1 through h6 tags are available for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but cannot use the associated Klasify element.

<h1>h1 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h1>
<h2>h2 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h2>
<h3>h3 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h3>
<h4>h4 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h4>
<h5>h5 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h5>
<h6>h6 I think I'm getting smaller by the second</h6>
